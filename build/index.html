What you get

Everything a serious reefer needs to log.

Built around what actually matters: accurate records, trends you can see, and alerts when something drifts outside the ranges you set.

Water Parameter Logging

Log all your parameters — alkalinity, calcium, magnesium, salinity, pH, temperature, nitrate, phosphate, and any custom parameters you define. Every reading timestamped, searchable, and chartable.

Trend Charts

See how each parameter moves over 7, 30, or 90 days. Spot a creeping alk swing before your corals do. Your logbook finally tells a visual story.

Maintenance & Dosing Scheduler

Set recurring routines — water changes, two-part dosing, testing, skimmer and filter cleaning — and ReefDeck tracks what's due and what's overdue. Optional browser reminders nudge you while the app is open or installed. You set the schedule; we just keep time.

Dose Calculator

Work out how much supplement raises alkalinity, calcium, or magnesium to your target — scaled to your exact water volume, pre-filled from your latest test. It does the arithmetic on the dose rate from your bottle; no recipes baked in, no guessing. Log the dose straight to your journal.

Your Threshold Alerts

Set your own acceptable range for each parameter. ReefDeck flags readings outside that range — no advice, no "dose X," just "this is outside the range you set." You decide what to do.

Coral & Livestock Inventory

Catalog every frag, fish, and invert. Add photos stored on your device, notes on placement and color, purchase date and price, and watch your collection grow over time.

Dosing & Maintenance Journal

Log every dose, water change, equipment service, and tank observation with a timestamp. No more trying to remember if you dosed two-part yesterday or the day before.

Export / Import (JSON + CSV)

Your data is yours. Export everything to JSON for a full backup or CSV to open in Excel. Import it back any time, on any device. You are never locked in.

Offline-Capable PWA

Install to your phone's home screen. Works in the fish room without Wi-Fi. Service worker caches the app — log readings while your router is rebooting.

Multi-Tank Support

Running a display tank, a frag tank, and a QT? Give each tank its own profile and switch between them instantly. All data stays separate and organized.

Light or Dark, Your Call

The deep Abyssal Green dark theme is built for dim fish rooms and late-night dosing. Prefer something brighter? Flip to the Ocean Mist light theme in Settings — same data, your choice, remembered on your device.

Your Google Drive Backup — Coming Pro

Currently in development: Pro adds one-tap encrypted backup to your own Google Drive, so your logbook survives a phone replacement and restores on any device. Your data stays in your Google account — we never run a sync server or keep a copy. Sign up to be notified when it ships.

Privacy by design

Your tank data never leaves your device.

ReefDeck is client-side first. We designed it this way intentionally — we do not want your water chemistry data, and we do not hold it. Everything lives in your browser's localStorage.

  • No account required to use the logbook
  • No data transmitted to any server in the free tier
  • No analytics on your tank readings
  • Export your full dataset any time, in open formats
  • Delete everything instantly — just clear browser storage
  • Works completely offline after first load

CoralVue HYDROS and Neptune Apex are excellent hardware controllers — but their software only works if you buy their equipment. ReefDeck works with any test kit, any controller, any tank. Zero hardware dependency.

Best Reef Tank App 2026

Tracking your frag collection, purchase prices, growth over time, and placement notes in a spreadsheet is painful. ReefDeck's inventory gives every coral its own card with photos and a full observation history.
