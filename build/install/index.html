Install ReefDeck on your device

ReefDeck is a web app, so there's nothing to download from an app store. Add it to your home screen once and it opens full-screen and works offline — just like a native app.

iPhone & iPad

Use Safari — Chrome on iOS can't add to the home screen.
  1. Open reefdeck.io/app in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button — the square with an arrow pointing up (↑) — at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down the share sheet and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add in the top-right corner. The ReefDeck icon appears on your home screen.
Don't see "Add to Home Screen"? Make sure you're in Safari, not another browser, and that you tapped the Share button (not the address bar).

Android

Works in Chrome, Edge, Samsung Internet, and most Android browsers.
  1. Open reefdeck.io/app in Chrome.
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu in the top-right corner.
  3. Tap Install app (some phones say Add to Home screen).
  4. Confirm Install. ReefDeck lands in your app drawer and home screen.
Chrome may also show a small "Install" banner at the bottom — tapping that does the same thing.

Desktop (Windows / Mac / Linux)

Use Chrome, Edge, or Brave.
  1. Open reefdeck.io/app in Chrome or Edge.
  2. Look at the right end of the address bar for the install icon — a small monitor with a down-arrow.
  3. Click it, then click Install.
  4. ReefDeck opens in its own window and pins to your taskbar or dock.
No install icon? Open the browser menu (⋮) → Cast, save, and share or Apps → Install this site as an app.

Why install?

  • Opens full-screen from your home screen — no browser bars in the way
  • Works offline: log parameters at the tank even with no signal
  • Faster to open, feels like a real app
  • Your data still lives on your device — installing changes nothing about privacy
